Maverick and his team successfully destroy the target, but encounter enemy planes and missiles during their mission. Maverick's plane is hit and he is presumed dead, but he miraculously survives and is rescued by his teammate. Despite being grounded permanently and replaced as an instructor, Maverick is appointed as the team leader after completing the mission. Later, Maverick and his teammate steal an F-14 aircraft and fly it to the carrier, proving that they can still fly.

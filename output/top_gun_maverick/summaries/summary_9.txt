Maverick and his team engage in a mission to destroy a target but encounter enemy planes and missiles. Maverick's plane is hit, and he is presumed dead but miraculously survives and is rescued by his teammate. Despite being grounded permanently and replaced as an instructor, Maverick is appointed as the team leader after completing the mission. Later, Maverick and his teammate steal an F-14 aircraft and fly it to the carrier, proving that they can still fly. In the midst of a dogfight, Maverick and his teammate take down enemy planes but are ultimately forced to eject from their own aircraft. Maverick survives the crash and is commended for his actions, while also coming to terms with the loss of his friend, Goose.
